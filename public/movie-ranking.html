The Best Movies According to Kyle

My top 3 movies of recent times

Avatar: The Way of the Water

A visully stunning film that uses state of the art CGI.

The Northman

I love viking history and this film puts that to good use with fanatasy elements. Has incredible cinematography too.

Baby Driver

A heartfelt story with drama, action and music. One second you're holding your butt in your seat, the next your trying your best to hold the tears back.
